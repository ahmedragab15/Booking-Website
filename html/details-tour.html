Amazon Rainforest Adventure

4.7 (312 reviews)

Manaus, Brazil

Tour Description

Embark on an unforgettable 5-day adventure through the heart of the Amazon rainforest. This small-group tour takes you deep into the jungle with expert naturalist guides, where you'll encounter exotic wildlife, learn about indigenous cultures, and experience the breathtaking biodiversity of the world's largest rainforest.

Tour Highlights

  • Canoe through flooded forests and spot pink river dolphins
  • Night jungle walk to discover nocturnal creatures
  • Visit a local indigenous community
  • Piranha fishing expedition
  • Sunrise birdwatching tour

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Manaus

Arrive in Manaus and transfer to our eco-lodge. Evening orientation and welcome dinner.

Day 2: Amazon River Exploration

Boat tour along the Amazon River, visit the Meeting of Waters, and afternoon jungle hike.
