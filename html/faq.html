Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an experience?

Booking is simple! Just browse our experiences, select your desired date and options, and proceed to checkout. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

What is your cancellation policy?

Most experiences can be cancelled for free up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Some special experiences may have different policies which will be clearly stated during booking.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! For groups of 6 or more, we offer special discounted rates. Contact our customer service team for more information and to arrange your group booking.

How do I contact customer support?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 via email at needhelp@gmail.com or by phone at 666 888 0000. You can also use the chat feature in our app.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Some regional payment methods may also be available.
