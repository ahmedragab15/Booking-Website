Our History

From humble beginnings to a global travel platform, here's our journey:

2010 - Founded

Booking was founded by travel enthusiasts Sarah Johnson and Michael Chen in a small office in San Francisco.

2012 - First Milestone

We reached 10,000 bookings and expanded our team to 15 employees.

2015 - Mobile App Launch

Launched our award-winning mobile app, making booking on the go easier than ever.

2018 - Global Expansion

Expanded our services to Europe and Asia, offering experiences in 50+ countries.

2020 - Pandemic Response

Implemented flexible booking policies to support travelers during challenging times.

2025 - Today

Serving over 1 million customers annually with 500+ unique travel experiences worldwide.
